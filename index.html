About Me

Jamie Jones is new to web development, beginning her career as a student in an online Bootcamp for web development. Before this, she was in the Army for a couple of years until she was medically discharged and returned home to Georgia. For a year, she worked at an animal hospital as a veterinary technician and kennel technician with the intention of getting experience before going to school to become a veterinarian. It wasn't long before she realized her true desire in life is to travel the world, and from a friend, she heard about web development, which would give her possibility while she still worked. After research, she decided this was something she would find enjoyable, and she quickly signed up for a three month web development course.

Jamie is an animal lover and adopted a three week old kitten, now named Ellie, while working at the animal hospital. She also has a 12 year old dog named Chanel. She loves horror movies and listening to Kpop as well as playing video games like and reading.
